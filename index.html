Fruit Merge Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters while you play.

This page explains what information Fruit Merge uses, how it is handled, and how you can contact us if you need help.

Last updated: June 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fruit Merge. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be used when you play our game and what choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

Fruit Merge is designed to use as little personal information as possible.

  • Gameplay data stored on your device: such as best score, settings, sound preferences, and similar local app data.
  • Support contact information: if you email us directly, we may receive your email address and the information you include in your message.
  • Device or diagnostic data: your device or app store platform may provide limited technical information if needed to keep the app stable and improve performance.
  • Advertising-related data: if ads are shown in the app, advertising partners may collect device identifiers, approximate location, app interaction data, and other information needed to provide, personalize, measure, or improve ads.

3. How We Use Information

  • To save your game settings and progress-related preferences on your device.
  • To respond to support requests and user questions.
  • To maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the game experience.
  • To display ads, limit ad fraud, and measure ad performance where advertising is enabled.

4. Ads and Third-Party Services

Fruit Merge may use third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect and process data such as advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, and app usage details in order to serve ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad effectiveness.

Google and its partners may use this information to show personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on your device settings, region, consent choices, and applicable law. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers, such as Google Play, Google AdMob, analytics or infrastructure partners, only when necessary to distribute the app, operate features, provide ads, process app-related services, or support app functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

Fruit Merge is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app itself. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support contact, please email us so we can review and remove it if needed.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information associated with the game and support requests. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the app. If you contact us by email, we may keep those communications only as long as needed to provide support and resolve issues. Third-party advertising providers may retain data according to their own policies and legal obligations.

9. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can remove locally stored app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.
  • You can contact us if you want support-related information reviewed or deleted where applicable.
  • You may be able to manage ad personalization through your device settings, Google account settings, or consent prompts shown in the app.
